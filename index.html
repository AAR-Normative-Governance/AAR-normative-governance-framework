AAR — Auto-Alignment Regulation

Normative Governance Framework

AAR (Auto-Alignment Regulation) is an independent normative governance framework for artificial intelligence and automated decision systems.

Its focus is pre-execution decision governance, including authority boundaries, deviation evaluation, and graduated containment in critical contexts.

AAR is not a product, not a service, and not a software system. Use is subject to licensing.
